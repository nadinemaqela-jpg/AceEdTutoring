AceEd Tutoring has a structured cancellation and refund policy to ensure fairness to both students and tutors.

Cancellation Policy

  • No-show: If a student does not attend a scheduled session without prior notice, the session will be considered completed and no refund or reschedule will be provided.

Refund Policy
