About AceEd Tutoring

Founded in 2024, AceEd Tutoring is dedicated to helping Grade 10 to 12 students excel in their studies. We understand the challenges of the CAPS curriculum and provide personalized support to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Our mission is to make quality education accessible and affordable for all students across South Africa. With experienced lead tutors in Bizana, Stellenbosch, and Durban, plus flexible online options, we're committed to your success.
