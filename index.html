About AceEd Tutoring

Founded in 2026, AceEd Tutoring is all about helping Grade 10 to 12 students do better in school and feel more confident in their studies. We understand how challenging the CAPS curriculum can be, so we offer personalized support to help every student reach their full potential.

Our goal is simple: to make quality education accessible and affordable for students across South Africa. With dedicated tutors in Bizana, Stellenbosch, and Durban, plus flexible online lessons, we’re here to support you every step of the way.
