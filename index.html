About AceEd Tutoring

Founded in 2024, AceEd Tutoring is dedicated to helping Grade 8 to 12 students excel in their studies. We understand the challenges of the CAPS curriculum and provide personalized support to ensure every student reaches their full potential.

Our mission is to make quality education accessible and affordable for all students in Bizana and surrounding areas. With experienced tutors and flexible learning options, we're committed to your success.
